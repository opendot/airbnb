Open Airbnb

rationale Conventionally referred to as the sharing economy "poster child", Airbnb has increasingly been at the centre of the debate regarding the social implications that such new, digitially enhanced market, might lead to. An increasing amount of evidence suggests in fact links to gentrification phenomena, with consequent increase in housing rents and pricing out of local communities. We tried to investigate if similar events were taking place also in Milan, Italy.
how we did it "Big Tech" companies are notoriously prone to collect users' data but conversely shy when asked to disclose them, especially those related to racial and gender issues. That's why we resorted to OpenAirbnb, a tool for scraping data directly from the Airbnb app. For more info on how we did, check the project page.
disclaimer None of the data, text and visualisation hereby reported suggests causal links between Airbnb and the aforementioned social issues. This is merely an exploratory piece and an exercise in storytelling through digital maps.
